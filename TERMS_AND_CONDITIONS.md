## 5. Accuracy of Sun & Shade Calculations

Smart Seating provides **estimated recommendations** based on solar position calculations. The accuracy of recommendations depends on:

- The accuracy of your entered origin and destination cities.
- Your actual departure time.
- Weather conditions (clouds, overcast skies are not factored in).
- Actual road curvature and bus route heading, which may differ from the straight-line bearing used in calculations.

**The App's recommendations are informational only.** We do not guarantee that the recommended seat will always be in shade. Actual sun exposure may vary based on real-world conditions outside our control.
